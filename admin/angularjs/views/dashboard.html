Company Profile

Company Description
Alliance Software, Inc. is a global IT services and solutions company, established in 2000. The company's delivery model is anchored on both on-shore and off-shore approach stressing on effective use of the strengths of people, process, and technology. As part of this strategic delivery model, Alliance has established offices in the key markets of Tokyo, Cebu and Manila.
